What Crema does

⌨️

Naps while you type

Curls up beside your work and sleeps until you stop typing.

👆

Sits when you click

Click Crema and it sits down to look at you.

✋

Drag it around

Move Crema anywhere. Won't wake it if it's asleep.

🗑️

Eats files

Drop a file on Crema — it eats it into the Trash (recoverable).

🖱️

Right-click to switch

Swap between cartoon & realistic looks anytime.

🪶

Featherweight

Native Swift. ~40MB RAM, near 0% CPU when idle. Not Electron.

How to install

Download Crema.dmg using the button above.

Double-click the downloaded Crema.dmg to open it.

Drag Crema into your Applications folder.

Open Applications and double-click Crema. Look at the bottom-left of your screen — there's your dog! 🐶

💡 Crema has no Dock or menu-bar icon — it's a pure desktop companion. To quit, right-click it and choose Quit.

Works on any Mac (Intel or Apple Silicon), macOS 14 or later.
