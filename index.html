How to install

🍎 Mac

Open Crema.dmg and drag Crema into your Applications folder.

Open Crema — your dog appears at the bottom-left of the screen. 🐶

🪟 Windows

Unzip the download and open Crema.exe.

If Windows shows a warning, click More info → Run anyway.

Your dog appears at the bottom-left. 🐶

💡 To quit, right-click Crema and choose Quit.
