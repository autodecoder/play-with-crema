How to install

Download Crema.dmg using the button above.

Double-click the downloaded Crema.dmg to open it.

Drag Crema into your Applications folder.

Open Applications and double-click Crema. Look at the bottom-left of your screen — there's your dog! 🐶

💡 Crema has no Dock or menu-bar icon — it's a pure desktop companion. To quit, right-click it and choose Quit.

Works on any Mac (Intel or Apple Silicon), macOS 14 or later.
